ข้อ 1–8 · Section 1
Affirmative — ประโยคบอกเล่า
โครงสร้าง
S + have / has + past participle
have ใช้กับ I · You · We · They  |  has ใช้กับ He · She · It · ชื่อเดียว
1.
Scientists → have discoveredhave discovered (discover) a potential treatment for one of the most challenging autoimmune diseases in recent years.
2.
The government → has introducedhas introduced (introduce) several innovative policies to improve public transportation across the country.
3.
She → has achievedhas achieved (achieve) remarkable results in her academic research throughout this academic year.
4.
They → have raisedhave raised (raise) millions of dollars to support vital environmental conservation projects around the world.
5.
He → has publishedhas published (publish) three highly influential books on sustainable development so far in his career.
6.
The organisation → has supportedhas supported (support) hundreds of disadvantaged families in remote rural communities over the years.
7.

8.
The committee → has approvedhas approved (approve) the new proposal for sustainable urban planning and development in the region.

ข้อ 24–31 · Section 4
ever & never
การใช้ ever และ never
ever — ใช้ในประโยคคำถาม
ใช้กับ Present Perfect เพื่อถามถึงประสบการณ์ที่เคยเกิดขึ้นในชีวิต ตำแหน่ง: หลัง have/has และก่อน V3
Have you ever visited Japan?
Has she ever won an award?
never — ใช้ในประโยคบอกเล่า (ความหมายปฏิเสธ)
ความหมาย "ไม่เคยเลยสักครั้ง" โครงสร้างเป็นบอกเล่า แต่มีความหมายปฏิเสธ ไม่ต้องใช้ not
I have never tried bungee jumping.
He has never broken a promise.
ตำแหน่งของทั้งคู่: S + have/has + ever/never + past participle
24.
Have you → everever visited a country where you were unable to speak the local language at all?
25.
She has → nevernever failed to meet a deadline throughout her entire professional career.
26.
Have you → everever participated in an international community volunteering programme abroad?
27.
He has → nevernever experienced any form of discrimination based on his cultural or ethnic background.
28.
Has the committee → everever rejected a funding proposal unanimously in the entire history of the institution?
29.
They have → nevernever considered abandoning the project despite facing serious financial and logistical difficulties.
30.
Have scientists → everever encountered a species this biologically complex in any previous expedition?
31.
She has → nevernever broken a promise to any of her students in over twenty years of dedicated teaching.
ข้อ 32–43 · Section 5
since & for
การใช้ since และ for
since — จุดเริ่มต้นของเวลา
ใช้บอกว่าสิ่งนั้นเริ่มต้นเมื่อไหร่ ตามด้วยจุดเวลาที่ระบุได้
since 2018 · since she was a child
since last Monday · since the project began
for — ระยะเวลาที่ผ่านไป
ใช้บอกระยะเวลาที่กินไป ตามด้วยช่วงเวลา
for three years · for a decade
for several months · for a long time
32.
He has worked for the organisation → forfor over twenty years without ever taking extended leave.
33.

34.
The government has been implementing the new education policy → forfor three academic terms with mixed results.
35.
They have not communicated formally with the international committee → sincesince last December.
36.
The researcher has dedicated her entire professional career to this field → forfor nearly a decade.
37.
He has not returned to his hometown → sincesince he graduated from university and moved abroad.
38.
The organisation has supported underprivileged communities → sincesince its establishment in 2001.
39.
She has been studying three foreign languages simultaneously → forfor more than six years.
40.
The infrastructure project has been completely on hold → sincesince the funding was unexpectedly withdrawn by the ministry.
41.
They have successfully collaborated on joint research → forfor a considerable period of time and produced impressive results.
42.
The patient has shown no significant signs of recovery → sincesince the new treatment regimen began last month.
43.
The conflict between the two neighbouring communities has continued → forfor several generations without resolution.
ข้อ 44–48 · Section 6
before
การใช้ before กับ Present Perfect
before ใช้กับ Present Perfect เพื่อบอกว่าสิ่งนั้นเคยเกิดขึ้นหรือไม่เคยเกิดขึ้น "ในอดีตก่อนนี้" โดยไม่ระบุเวลาที่ชัดเจน
ตำแหน่งของ before
มักวางไว้ท้ายประโยค
I have seen this film before.
She has never done this before.
Have you been here before?
ข้อ 44–45: เติม V3
ใส่ past participle ที่ถูกต้องในประโยคที่มี before อยู่แล้ว
ข้อ 46–48: เติม before
เลือกใส่ "before" ให้ถูกตำแหน่ง
