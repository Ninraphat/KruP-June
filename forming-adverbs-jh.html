ข้อ 1–8 · Rule 1
Regular Adjective + -ly
กฎการสร้าง
quick→quickly
careful→carefully
significant→significantly
gradual→gradually
หมายเหตุ: adjective ที่ลงท้ายด้วย -ll เติมเพียง -y เช่น full → fully · dull → dully
1.
She spoke → confidentlyconfidently (confident) to the entire audience throughout her research presentation.
2.
The scientist → carefullycarefully (careful) examined each sample under the high-powered microscope.
3.
The population of the coastal city has → graduallygradually (gradual) increased over the past decade.
4.
He → efficientlyefficiently (efficient) managed the project and delivered all results ahead of the original schedule.
5.
The new policy → significantlysignificantly (significant) reduced the level of air pollution across the entire region.
6.
She → persistentlypersistently (persistent) worked towards her academic goal despite facing many difficult obstacles.
7.
The team → remarkablyremarkably (remarkable) recovered from their early defeat to win the national championship.
8.
The new medical technology → considerablyconsiderably (considerable) improved the quality of healthcare services in rural areas.
ข้อ 9–14 · Rule 2
Adjective ending -y → -ily
กฎการสร้าง — ตัด -y แล้วเติม -ily
happy→happily
easy→easily
heavy→heavily
angry→angrily
9.
The advanced students → easilyeasily (easy) solved the complex mathematical problem in just a few minutes.
10.
It → heavilyheavily (heavy) rained throughout the night, which caused severe flooding across the city.
11.
He → angrilyangrily (angry) responded to the journalist's unfair criticism about his latest research findings.
12.
She → happilyhappily (happy) accepted the prestigious scholarship offered by the international university.
13.
The patient recovered → steadilysteadily (steady) after the successful surgical operation performed by the specialist.
14.
The volunteers → readilyreadily (ready) offered their assistance to the victims of the natural disaster.
ข้อ 15–20 · Rule 3
Adjective ending -le → drop -e, add -y
กฎการสร้าง — ตัด -le แล้วเติม -ly
comfortable→comfortably
gentle→gently
terrible→terribly
simple→simply
15.
She → comfortablycomfortably (comfortable) settled into her new role as the head of the research department.
16.
The earthquake → terriblyterribly (terrible) devastated the local communities along the entire coastline.
17.
The experienced teacher → gentlygently (gentle) explained the complex scientific concept to the confused students.
18.
The committee → responsiblyresponsibly (responsible) managed the funds donated by several international organisations.
19.
He argued his position → capablycapably (capable) in front of the review panel, addressing each point with strong evidence.
20.
The solution to the conflict was → simplysimply (simple) achieved through open and honest communication between both sides.
ข้อ 21–25 · Rule 4
Adjective ending -ic → -ically
กฎการสร้าง — เติม -ally ต่อจาก -ic
dramatic→dramatically
automatic→automatically
drastic→drastically
enthusiastic→enthusiastically
ยกเว้น: public → publicly (ไม่ใช่ publically)
21.
The political situation → dramaticallydramatically (dramatic) changed after the unexpected result of the national election.
22.
The security door locks → automaticallyautomatically (automatic) when the last employee leaves the building each evening.
23.
She → enthusiasticallyenthusiastically (enthusiastic) participated in every workshop and seminar during the intensive training programme.
24.
He → realisticallyrealistically (realistic) assessed all the potential challenges before making his final strategic decision.
25.
The new water-saving technology → drasticallydrastically (drastic) reduced consumption levels in drought-affected communities.
ข้อ 26–32 · Rule 5
Irregular & Same-Form Adverbs
รูปแบบพิเศษ — ไม่ได้เติม -ly
good→well(irregular)
hard→hard(same)
fast→fast(same)
late→late(same)
early→early(same)
high→high(same)
deep→deep(same)
ระวัง: hard → hardly = "แทบจะไม่" · late → lately = "เมื่อเร็วๆ นี้" · high → highly = "อย่างมาก (นามธรรม)"
26.
She performed → wellwell (good) in all her university entrance examinations and received the top scholarship.
27.
The athletes trained → hardhard (hard) throughout the entire season to prepare for the national championship.
28.
He worked → latelate (late) into the night to complete the critical sections of his research report.
29.
The new train departed → earlyearly (early) because all passengers had already boarded ahead of the scheduled time.
30.
The eagle soared → highhigh (high) above the mountain range while searching for prey in the valley below.
31.
The electric sports car accelerated → fastfast (fast) and disappeared beyond the horizon within seconds.
32.
The marine biologist dived → deepdeep (deep) into the ocean to photograph the rare coral formations on the seabed.
ข้อ 33–40 · Section 6
Mixed Challenge — ทุก Rule
คำแนะนำ
ดูคำ adjective ในวงเล็บแล้วตัดสินใจว่าต้องใช้กฎข้อใด ก่อนเติมรูป adverb ที่ถูกต้อง
33.
The senator → bravelybravely (brave) challenged the corrupt policy in front of the entire parliament.
34.
The rescue team → immediatelyimmediately (immediate) responded to the emergency situation in the flooded district.
35.
The surgeon → preciselyprecisely (precise) performed the delicate procedure without making a single error.
36.
She → absolutelyabsolutely (absolute) refused to accept the unjust conditions imposed by the organisation on its workers.
37.
The young architect → creativelycreatively (creative) designed a public library that harmonised beautifully with its natural surroundings.
38.
The medical staff worked → tirelesslytirelessly (tireless) throughout the night to treat all the injured accident victims.
39.
The final results turned out → surprisinglysurprisingly (surprising) positive, far exceeding all of the researchers' original expectations.
40.
The restoration project was → nearlynearly (near) complete when the funding was unexpectedly withdrawn by the government.
