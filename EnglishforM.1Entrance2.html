Exam Strategy

Top Tips for ม.1 Entrance Exams

Applied across all 8 units — these strategies appear in every unit's Review section.

🎯
Read the whole sentence first. Don't fill in the blank until you understand the full meaning and context.
🔍
Identify the verb form. Most questions test tense, voice, modal, or reported form — look at ALL verbs.
⚡
Use elimination. Cross out obvious wrong answers first. 2-choice is much easier than 4-choice.
⏰
Time signal words are clues. yesterday/ago = past, tomorrow/next = future, since/for = perfect tense.
🚫
Watch for double negatives. "Unless you don't study" = wrong! "Unless" already means "if not".
📌
Commas matter. A comma before "which/who" = non-defining clause → "that" is forbidden.
🔄
Check active vs. passive. Does the subject do the action or receive it? This determines active or passive voice.
💬
Reported speech word order. Never use question word order after "asked" — always Subject + Verb.
📝
Learn the "exam trap" lists. Each unit has 8 top traps. These are the most commonly tested errors.
